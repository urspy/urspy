Follow the rules

List of all Minecraft and Discord server rules.

Ignorance of the rules is no excuse!

Minecraft Server Rules

  1. Be respectful to all players. No harassment or discrimination.
  2. No griefing or stealing from other players.
  3. Do not use hacks, cheats, or mods that give an unfair advantage.
  4. Keep chat family-friendly. No profanity or inappropriate content.
  5. Follow the instructions of staff and moderators at all times.
  6. Do not spam the chat or advertise other servers.
  7. Build responsibly and do not disrupt others’ creations.
  8. Have fun and contribute positively to the community!

Discord Server Rules

  1. Be respectful to all members. Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination is not allowed.
  2. Keep discussions appropriate. Avoid NSFW, offensive, or inappropriate content.
  3. No spamming, flooding chat, or using excessive emojis or caps.
  4. Do not advertise or self-promote without permission from moderators.
  5. Follow all Discord Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
  6. Do not impersonate other members or staff.
  7. Use the appropriate channels for specific topics.
  8. Listen to the moderators and follow their instructions at all times.

More Rules

  1. No sharing personal information about yourself or others.
  2. Do not engage in political, religious, or controversial discussions.
  3. Avoid tagging staff unnecessarily unless it’s an emergency.
  4. Do not abuse bots or exploit server features.
  5. English is the primary language of this server; please use it in public chats.
  6. Do not bypass server filters or restrictions.
  7. Report any rule violations to moderators privately.
  8. Maintain a positive and welcoming atmosphere for everyone.

If you have read the rules, you can go and enjoy the fun on our server!
