WARZONE COMBAT RULES

Violations will be met with immediate consequences

Core Combat Regulations

  • 1
    No Cross-Teaming Permaban
    Alliances between different teams are strictly prohibited. Coordinate only with your registered teammates.
  • 2
    No Hacks or Exploits Permaban
    X-ray, KillAura, reach hacks, macros, autoclickers, or any unfair advantage modifications are prohibited.
  • 3
    Team Size Limit (5 Players) DQ
    No substitutions or mid-event joins allowed. Your registered squad is final when WAR begins.
  • 4
    No Combat Logging DQ
    Disconnecting during PvP engagement will result in automatic disqualification.
  • 5
    No Lag Machines Permaban
    Any attempt to crash or lag the server will result in permanent blacklisting.

Voice & Communication

  • 6
    Mandatory Voice Chat Kick
    All participants must join the designated WARZONE voice channel for coordination.
  • 7
    No Voice Chat Abuse Mute
    Keep comms clear of spam, toxicity, or excessive noise. Strategic communication only.

Equipment Restrictions

  • 8
    No Crystal PvP DQ
    End crystals and crystal-based combat tactics are strictly forbidden in WARZONE events.
  • 9
    Approved Mods Only DQ
    Only FPS/performance mods (Optifine, Sodium) allowed. Anything giving competitive advantage is banned.

Combat Zones

  • 10
    No Safe Zones Warning
    All base protections are disabled when WAR begins. Every area is a combat zone.
  • 11
    No End War Tactics DQ
    Deliberately prolonging battles or avoiding engagement to stall the war is prohibited.

Conduct & Sportsmanship

  • 12
    Respect All Participants Kick
    No toxicity, hate speech, or harassment. Keep trash talk minimal and good-natured.
  • 13
    Follow Staff Instructions DQ
    Refusal to comply with staff directives will result in immediate removal.

WARZONE COMMAND DECREE: SpyMC staff reserves full authority to disqualify or ban any player/team violating these rules. All decisions are final. Play with honor, fight with skill, and may the best team prevail.
