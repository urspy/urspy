⚔️ SpyMC ENDWAR Rules

Read carefully. Break the rules, face the consequence.

  • 1. No Cross-Teaming: Only fight with your team. No outside help.
  • 2. No Hacks or Exploits: X-ray, KillAura, macros, autoclickers = instant ban.
  • 3. Max 5 Players per Team: No subs or mid-event joins. Your squad is final.
  • 4. No Combat Logging: If you log during PvP, you’re out.
  • 5. No Lag Machines or Server Crashing: Attempt = Permanent Ban.
  • 6. Respect Staff & Opponents: No toxicity, spam, or hate speech. Stay clean.
  • 7. Allowed Mods Only: FPS mods like Optifine are okay. Advantage mods are not.
  • 8. Base Protection Ends When WAR Starts: No safe zones. No mercy. Only chaos.

SpyMC Staff has full rights to disqualify or ban any player or team breaking the rules.
Play fair. Fight smart. Survive the storm.
