RESPONSE TIMES

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. Discord support typically has faster response times during staff availability hours.

What to Include in Your Support Request

To help us assist you faster, please include the following information in your support ticket:

  • Your Minecraft username: The primary account you use on our server
  • Detailed description: Explain your issue or question thoroughly
  • Screenshots: If applicable, include screenshots of the issue
  • Error messages: Copy any error messages you've received
  • Time and date: When the issue occurred or started
PRO TIP

For ban appeals, include your username, reason for ban (if known), and a thoughtful explanation of why you should be unbanned.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a player who is breaking rules?

Use the /report command in-game if available, or provide evidence (screenshots/video) through our Discord server in the appropriate reporting channel.

I lost my items due to a bug. Can I get them back?

We generally don't restore lost items except in cases of verified server-side issues. Contact us with details about what happened and when.

How do I purchase ranks or cosmetics?

Visit our web store at www.spymc.xyz. For payment issues, contact support@spymc.xyz with your transaction details.

My account was hacked. What should I do?

Contact us immediately at support@spymc.xyz from your registered email address. We'll secure your account and help you regain access.
