Anti-Cheat & Exploit Prevention

Our custom-built anti-cheat system works in tandem with the 100ms/net protocol to detect and prevent cheating in real-time.

  • Movement Analysis: Detects unnatural movement patterns, flight, speed hacking
  • Combat Verification: Validates combat actions for impossible reach, autoclicking, or aim assistance
  • Inventory Monitoring: Prevents item duplication, unauthorized item generation, or inventory manipulation
  • Interaction Validation: Ensures all player interactions with the world follow game mechanics
  • Client Integrity Checks: Verifies client modifications are within allowed parameters

  • Allow reasonable time for us to address the issue before making it public
