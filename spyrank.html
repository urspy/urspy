Primary Commands

!spy rank <IGN>

The most important command that grants you the SPY rank in-game. This is a one-time use command that permanently links your Minecraft account to your Discord.

Example

Correct: !spy rank Agent007

Incorrect: !spy rank agent007 (if your actual IGN is Agent007)

Important

Your IGN must match exactly including:

  • Capitalization (case-sensitive)
  • Special characters
  • No extra spaces before or after
There is no way to change this link once established.

!spy link

Verifies your current linked Minecraft account and displays:

  • Your connected Minecraft IGN
  • Date and time of linking
  • SPY rank status (active/inactive)
!spy help

Displays an interactive help menu with:

  • All available commands

System Requirements & Restrictions

Eligibility Criteria

  1. Your Discord account must be at least 7 days old
  2. You must have the Verified role in our Discord
  3. Your Minecraft account must have joined our server at least once
  4. No active bans or mutes on either platform
Strict Prohibitions

The following will result in immediate removal of your SPY rank and possible bans:

  • Attempting to link multiple Discord accounts to one Minecraft account
  • Trying to bypass the one-time use restriction
  • Using special characters to impersonate others
  • Sharing account access that leads to rank abuse
